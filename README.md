MySaathi 🌸

AI-Based Menstrual Cycle Tracking App

MySaathi is an AI-powered menstrual cycle tracking application designed to help women monitor periods, predict cycles, track symptoms, and gain meaningful insights into reproductive health. The app focuses on accuracy, privacy, and accessibility.

🚀 Features

📅 Menstrual cycle tracking & smart predictions

🤖 AI-based pattern analysis

🌸 Ovulation & fertile window insights

😌 PMS, mood & symptom tracking

🔔 Period & health reminders

🔐 Privacy-first approach (data security focused)

🎯 Objective

MySaathi aims to reduce stigma around menstrual health and provide women with a reliable digital companion for informed health decisions using technology and data-driven insights.

🛠 Tech Stack

Frontend: Flutter

Backend: Firebase / REST APIs

AI Logic: Data analysis & cycle prediction models

📱 Target Users

Women seeking accurate cycle tracking

Users looking for health insights & reminders

Individuals prioritizing privacy in health apps

🔒 Privacy & Disclaimer

MySaathi does not replace medical advice.
All health insights are informational only. User data security and privacy are top priorities.

🌍 Vision

To build a globally trusted women’s health platform that combines technology, empathy, and accessibility—especially for users in developing regions.
